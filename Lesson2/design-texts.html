Contrast

The ultimate guitar page has good contrast, it makes the page look attractive, comfortable in the eyes of the users, the combination of black and yellow has always seemed excellent to me, plus the links and titles add a good color design to the page, not too much. bright but not so dark.

PARC: Proximity

On the Church page there is something very interesting and they are the links that use the proximity tool, this helps a lot in the mobile version since the user will have quick access to the links of the main topics of the page. , these are together and at first glance which is very good.

Ortizo Musical Page

Ortizo Official Page


White Space and Clean Design

The Ortizo's page of musical instruments has an excellent design, very comfortable to navigate looking for the product you want since the blank spaces or negative calls give an excellent view of what the page offers, it does not distract from the main point that In this case, it is the product and rather pay attention to it only, I really liked this page design and in the mobile version is excellent.
