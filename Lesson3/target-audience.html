Description target audience

This website is for everyone, the content is open for an audience of all ages, commonly our recurring visitors are adult persons that want to know the Idaho weather to develop their daily activities, which can be, the traffic state, which clothes they can use for the specific day or week if the weather will affect any project or activity that will take place outdoors, etc.

Five scenerios

According to the information I got from the two aforementioned people and asking certain questions for the purpose of my climate site and the different scenarios that people like them might have when visiting my page, the questions or possible scenarios would be the following:

  1. Can the site tell me what the traffic will be like according to the weather?
  2. can this site link to social media to provide information?
  3. Could this page tell me the exact time slot when the weather will change?
  4. Can this site provide recommendations on the weather according to my activities like work and study?
  5. Can this site give momentous news of how climate is affecting the entire world?
