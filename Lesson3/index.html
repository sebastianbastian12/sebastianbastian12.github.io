Purpose site

Predictyourday.com is a site that will help the people know the Idaho weather, the page will show the different weather states in the exact day and the exact time that the user will want to know. The site will do a summary of the past days' weather and predict the following ones giving respective recommendations to the users according to this information for their daily activities.
